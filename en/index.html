Comprehensive services

Customs clearance

Transport on T1 or TIR carnet? Goods straight off the train, maybe to England? No problem. We carry out customs loads every day.

ADR (dangerous goods)

ADR (Dangerous Goods): We handle material shipments dangerous in all categories except Class 7 and 1 . Check what we can offer regarding your ADR shipment.

SENT

Our dedicated specialist will help you carry out all of them formalities related to the transport of materials covered by the procedure SENT.

Fix Delivery

Fix deliveries are our specialty. Aware of the consequences resulting from failure to exercise due diligence in the course time-critical transports, we devote to these transports especially a lot of attention.

Consulting and benchmarking

Comprehensive managerial and technical support in the field planning and transport projects.

GPS

Our vehicles have a GPS monitoring system.

Pre-advised

We guarantee you a comprehensive service of your needs in the field organization of transport, including notification of collection and delivery your shipment, and booking time slots, if any required by you or by your contractors.

Time-critical transportation

We monitor every stage in the transport of these shipments on an ongoing basis, incl for which the guarantee of timeliness is a key requirement.

Insurance

At the request of our customers, for the duration of the transport we cover their goods with additional cargo insurance.

Offer

Thanks to our own fleet, we carry out road transport to each of the countries of Europe. Our offer includes:

  • Standard 13.6 m semi-trailers with a load capacity of up to 24 tons.

  • Spatial sets with increased cubic capacity - up to 120 cubic meters.

  • Mega trailers with a height of up to 3 meters.

  • Refrigerated and isothermal semi-trailers, with ADR equipment on the needs of the transport of dangerous goods.

  • Tipper semi-trailers.

  • Solos ideal for loads up to maximum 18 pallets or entry restrictions up to 8.5 tonnes.

  • Buses with a load capacity of up to 1 ton and a capacity of up to 10 seats pallet. Thanks to the lack of tachographs, this is the solution ideal for express shipments.
